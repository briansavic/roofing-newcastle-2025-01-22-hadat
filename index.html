About Roofing Newcastle

Founded in 2010, Roofing Newcastle began with a vision to revolutionize the roofing industry through sustainable practices and innovative technology. Our founder, drawing from 25 years of construction experience, assembled a team of certified experts committed to delivering exceptional quality and service.

Our mission is to provide environmentally responsible roofing solutions that protect homes while reducing environmental impact. We're driven by core values of sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction, ensuring every project meets our exacting standards for quality and efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roof installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 2-3 days, weather permitting. Large or complex projects may take 4-5 days. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

What warranty coverage do you provide?

Our comprehensive 30-year warranty covers materials and workmanship. It includes annual inspections and 24/7 emergency support.

How much can I save on energy costs?

Customers typically see 25-40% reduction in cooling costs and 15-30% in heating costs. Actual savings vary based on home size and climate conditions.

Can you integrate solar panels with your roofing systems?

Yes, our roofing systems are designed for seamless solar integration. We offer full solar installation services and can upgrade existing systems.

What financing options are available?

We offer flexible financing with terms up to 10 years, competitive rates, and $0 down payment options for qualified customers.
